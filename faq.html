FAQ

Everything you need to know about the services and others. Here are some most asked question answer for you.

The American Dental Association says you should ideally visit your dentist for a dental checkup and professional teeth cleaning at least twice every year to maintain optimum oral health.

When you visit One Health Dental for the first time, we will gather your details, including medical and dental history. Then, one of our doctors will give you a comprehensive oral exam, perform teeth cleaning if it's due, screen you for oral cancer, and obtain your dental X-rays if necessary. Once your dental exams are complete, we will discuss the diagnosis with you and determine the best course of action. And we will refer you to qualified specialists if needed. During your dental visits, you are free to ask any questions and doubts and discuss anything you feel relevant.

At One Health Dental, our doctors try hard to help our patients struggling with dental anxiety by making all our dental treatments as painless and quick as possible. Before each procedure, we will discuss all aspects of your treatment with you, take steps to alleviate the possibility of any pain and ensure that you stay comfortable and relaxed throughout the dental procedure.

We offer in-office teeth whitening and at-home bleaching under dental guidance to eliminate your dental stains and help you achieve a beautiful, bright smile. It is one of the most sought-after cosmetic procedures at our dental office. We recommend our patients undergo teeth whitening treatment under expert supervision to make it completely safe and ensure maximum results.

At One Health Dental, we are committed to providing the most suitable orthodontic treatments that suit our patients' needs. We will start by evaluating the patient's bite, jaw alignment, and entire mouth and determine the best treatment that suits their dental goals. We provide Traditional Metal Braces, Invisible Ceramic Braces, and Clear Orthodontic Aligners at our dental office.

One Health Dental offers comprehensive dental care to all your family members of all ages. Our dentists love seeing children of all ages and provide treatment to address their dental needs at every stage in their life.

We recommend you take good care of your child's oral health from their birth and schedule their dental appointment with us as soon as they get their first tooth or at one year of age, whichever comes first.

One or more missing teeth can affect your oral health and self-confidence. At One Health Dental, we offer tooth replacement options like dental implants, dentures, dental crowns, and bridges to replace your missing teeth and help you regain a healthy and fully-functional smile.

The dental experts at One Health Dental recommend these tips for our patients to maintain good oral health:

  • Brush your teeth for at least two minutes using a soft-bristled toothbrush and fluoride toothpaste twice a day.

  • Floss at least once a day.

  • Rinse your mouth with water after every meal.

  • Limit your sugar intake.

  • Have a balanced, nutritious diet.

  • Chew sugarless gum after a meal.

  • Never skip your regular dental visits.

We know a dental emergency can occur to anyone at any time. In case you have a dental emergency, call us immediately, and we will guide you on the first-aid steps you should take before you reach our dental office. We will also schedule your appointment with us as soon as possible so that you do not have to wait long to receive immediate dental attention.
