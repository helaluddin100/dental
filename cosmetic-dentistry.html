What Is Cosmetic Dentistry?

Cosmetic dentistry has witnessed an immense rise and popularity in recent years. Many people are finding different ways and means to correct their smiles and improve their aesthetic appearance. After all, a good smile creates a lasting impression and makes you feel confident about your personality.

Book Now

You may schedule an appointment with us for a suitable cosmetic dental procedure and achieve a desirable smile.

eeth Whitening

Professional teeth whitening is the most popular cosmetic dental procedure we perform at our clinic. The treatment ensures that you leave our office with a captivating smile. For this procedure, we apply a hydrogen peroxide gel on the surface of your teeth, keep it for some time and then wash it thoroughly.
The results of this procedure are immediate. You will notice that your smile has become up to eight shades lighter. Visit us for this procedure once every six months to maintain your pearly whites.

orcelain Veneers

Porcelain veneers solve many aesthetic dental concerns ‚Äì chipped teeth, broken teeth, minutely fractured teeth, gaps between your teeth, badly stained teeth, and so on. They are thin wafer-like shells that are placed on the front surface of your teeth. Veneers are customized according to the shape, size, and length of your teeth, thus fitting perfectly. They do not get stained and last long. These veneers are easy to clean and maintain.

ental Implants

Dental implants are a permanent treatment for your missing teeth. They are titanium studs acting as your artificial tooth root. We insert them in the place of your missing teeth, and they form an anchor for your dental crowns.

Owing to its numerous advantages, many people are opting for implants over dentures. They are easy to care for and maintain. They feel, function, and appear like your natural teeth.

ental Crowns

Dental crowns are a popular restorative option for cracked, chipped, misshapen, or badly decayed teeth. They are dental caps that we create to fit your teeth. Crowns are made using acrylic, porcelain, porcelain fused to metal, ceramic, and other materials. Nowadays, same-day crowns are also made using CAD-CAM technology.

ooth Shaping

Bonding is performed on broken, chipped, and badly-stained teeth. During the procedure, we attach a tooth-colored material to your affected teeth. It is a non-invasive procedure that improves the appearance of your teeth in the long run.
